I am honored to have been selected as one of fifty 2024 Udall Scholars! This is an amazing opportunity, and I look forward to attending the scholarship orientation in August. Thanks to the Udall Foundation, UT Jackson School of Geosciences, and The University of Texas at Austin Student Government for providing me with the experience, opportunity, and knowledge to begin pursuing and understanding the complex world of environmental science through both research and advocacy.

In August I visited Tucson, AZ, and got to know my cohort, learning about a variety of different topics — from the value of Indigenous Traditional Ecological Knowledge, to the importance of consensus in solving complex problems. I am thankful for this opportunity and to meet all the amazing people who are so passionate about so many different issues.
