I attended the Texas Climate Conference at Rice University with fellow UT students, where the state's leading climate scientists came together to talk about the exciting science they are doing. I presented my poster on using tree rings to reconstruct the history of droughts in Texas, and met other young scientists who are passionate about Texas climate!
