Hi y'all! I'm Will, a research hydrogeologist at the Bureau of Economic Geology and recent graduate of the University Texas at Austin. I work mostly on karst hydrogeology with a focus on surface-water/groundwater interaction across Texas. My undergraduate work focused on how we can infer groundwater flow paths based on the differential response of tree rings in different hydrogeologic settings to different climate conditions. I'm passionate about water resources and geology, and use a wide array of scientific tools in my research. In my free time I can usually be found frolicking in the Texas Hill Country, rock climbing, caving, hiking, paddling, and fishing.
